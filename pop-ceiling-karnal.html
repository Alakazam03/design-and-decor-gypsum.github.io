What is POP Ceiling?

POP (Plaster of Paris) ceiling is a traditional ceiling finishing technique that uses gypsum plaster to create smooth, seamless surfaces with decorative elements. POP ceilings are known for their ability to create intricate designs, patterns, cornices, and moldings that add elegance and character to any space.

In Karnal, POP ceilings remain a popular choice for homeowners who prefer traditional aesthetics with decorative elements. They offer a smooth finish, excellent for painting, and can be molded into various shapes and patterns to match your interior design theme.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does POP ceiling installation take?

Simple POP ceiling takes 2-3 days for a 200-300 sq.ft room. Decorative patterns and cornices may take 4-6 days depending on complexity. We'll provide a timeline during the free site visit.

Can POP ceiling be painted?

Yes! POP ceilings provide an excellent surface for painting. They can be painted with any color of your choice, and the smooth finish ensures even paint application.

Is POP ceiling better than gypsum ceiling?

Both have their advantages. POP is better for decorative elements, patterns, and traditional designs. Gypsum is better for modern designs with LED integration. We can help you choose based on your requirements.

Do POP ceilings crack?

When installed properly with quality materials and expert workmanship, POP ceilings are durable and crack-resistant. We use premium materials and proper techniques to ensure long-lasting results.

Can you create custom POP designs?

Absolutely! Our skilled artisans can create custom patterns, designs, and decorative elements as per your requirements. We'll discuss design options during the free site visit.
