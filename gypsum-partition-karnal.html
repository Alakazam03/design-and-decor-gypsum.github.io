What is Gypsum Partition?

Gypsum partition, also known as drywall partition, is a modern wall construction method using gypsum boards fixed to metal frames. These partitions are used to divide spaces in offices, homes, and commercial buildings without the need for traditional brick and mortar construction.

In Karnal, gypsum partitions have become the preferred choice for office spaces, home renovations, and commercial establishments due to their quick installation, sound insulation properties, and flexibility in design.

Types of Gypsum Partitions We Install in Karnal

1. Single Layer Gypsum Partition

Standard single-layer gypsum board partition suitable for most office and home applications. Cost-effective and quick to install.

  • Standard thickness: 4-6 inches
  • Basic sound insulation
  • Quick installation
  • Budget-friendly option

2. Double Layer Gypsum Partition

Enhanced partition with double gypsum boards on both sides for better sound insulation and durability. Ideal for offices and conference rooms.

  • Superior sound insulation
  • Enhanced durability
  • Better fire resistance
  • Ideal for offices and commercial spaces

3. Acoustic Gypsum Partition

Specialized partition with acoustic insulation material between gypsum boards. Perfect for recording studios, conference rooms, and spaces requiring soundproofing.

  • Excellent soundproofing
  • Reduces noise transmission
  • Ideal for offices and studios
  • Professional-grade installation

4. Fire-Rated Gypsum Partition

Fire-resistant partition using special fire-rated gypsum boards. Required for commercial buildings and provides enhanced safety.

  • Fire-resistant materials
  • Complies with safety standards
  • Required for commercial spaces
  • Enhanced building safety

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does gypsum partition installation take?

A standard gypsum partition for a 100-150 sq.ft area takes 2-3 days. Complex installations with multiple partitions may take 4-5 days. We'll provide a timeline during the free site visit.

Can gypsum partitions support heavy items?

Yes, with proper reinforcement. We can install additional support for hanging TVs, shelves, or cabinets. We'll assess your requirements during the site visit.

Are gypsum partitions waterproof?

Standard gypsum partitions are not waterproof. However, we can use moisture-resistant gypsum boards for areas like bathrooms and kitchens.

Can doors and windows be installed in gypsum partitions?

Absolutely! We can create cutouts for doors, windows, and electrical points. All openings are properly reinforced for durability.

Do you provide warranty on gypsum partition work?

Yes, all our gypsum partition installations come with a service warranty. We ensure quality workmanship and use premium materials for long-lasting results.
